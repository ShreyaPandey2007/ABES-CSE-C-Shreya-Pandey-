Welcome to ABESEC

ABES Engineering College (ABESEC), Ghaziabad, established in 2000, is a private institution affiliated with Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU), Lucknow. Located at 19th KM Stone, NH-09, Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh, it is approved by AICTE, accredited by NAAC with an A grade, and has NBA accreditation for select programs. The 17-acre campus is ranked 201â€“300 in the NIRF Engineering category for 2024. ABESEC offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in Engineering, Management, and Computer Applications, with popular courses including BTech, MTech, MBA, and MCA. Admissions are primarily entrance-based, requiring scores from exams like JEE Main, CUET UG, CUET PG, GATE, or UPCET, followed by UPTAC counseling. The college has a strong placement record, with a 78.57% placement rate in 2024, a highest package of INR 59.91 LPA, and an average package of INR 5.27 LPA.
